Understanding Primigravida

A primigravida is a woman who is pregnant for the first time. This term is often used in medical contexts to describe a first-time pregnancy, as it can have implications for both the mother and the developing fetus. It's essential for primigravidas to receive proper prenatal care to ensure the health and wellbeing of both mother and baby.
