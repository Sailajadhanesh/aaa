Girl Child

Empowering the girl child is essential for creating a balanced society. It includes providing equal access to education, healthcare, and opportunities for growth and development. Investing in girls can yield significant returns, contributing to healthier families, communities, and economies. They need balanced nutrition as they are in their growing stage. The nutritional needs are mentioned below. Fitness and growth are essential aspects of a girl child's overall development, impacting both physical and mental well-being. Focusing on fitness from a young age promotes healthy growth, confidence, and long-term health benefits.



Nutritional Needs for the Girl Child

  • Ensure a diet rich in fruits and vegetables

  • Include whole grains for energy.

  • Provide adequate protein sources, such as beans and nuts.

  • Incorporate dairy products for calcium and vitamin D

  • Encourage hydration and limit sugary drinks

  • Educate about healthy eating habits

  • Encourage the intake of roughage and dietary fiber.

  • Limit starchy foods.

  • Provide a limited amount of fats and carbohydrates.

Physical Exercise is compulsory for proper growth and fitness
