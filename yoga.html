Understanding Yoga

Yoga is a practice that connects the body, breath, and mind. It uses physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation to improve overall health. Yoga was developed as a spiritual practice thousands of years ago.





Yoga Poses for Different Groups

  • Pregnant Women

  • Cat-Cow Stretch: Helps relieve back pain.


  • Child's Pose: A restful position that can ease tension.


  • Seated Forward Bend: A gentle stretch for the lower back.


  • ide-Lying Positions: Great for relaxation and stability.


Working Women

Desk Stretch: Relieves tension from sitting for long periods


Chair Pose: Strengthens the legs and improves posture


Neck Rolls: Releases neck tension and improves circulation..


Reclined Bound Angle Pose: A restorative pose to relieve stress.


House Wives

● Downward-Facing Dog: Strengthens the whole body and energizes.

● Bridge Pose: Opens the chest and stretches the spine.

● Pigeon Pose: Opens the hips and relieves lower back pain.

● Legs-Up-the-Wall Pose: Promotes relaxation and reduces fatigue.
